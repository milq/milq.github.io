It's not about how good it looks flipped. It's the realization of how much your eyes and mind have gotten used to one side that only by flipping can you spot what you've missed.
Our mind is really good at compensating, so if you stare at an image for too long you may not actually see what it is, but rather what it should be.
So that's why you flip the canvas, or take a break and come back.
